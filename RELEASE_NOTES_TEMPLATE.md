## What WallCraft Pro does
WallCraft Pro creates, splits, and applies wallpapers across mixed monitor setups on Windows 10/11 without cropping or distorting the original images.

## Download

## Notes
- Supports mixed monitor sizes and alignments.
- Includes six layout modes.
- Can export, split by screen, or apply directly on Windows.
